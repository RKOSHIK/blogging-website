The VERO MODA Sale you wont want to miss

by Nat Wednesday, July 4, 2018

I love this time of the year not only because I love rains (well duh!) but the fact that I can get my hands on stunning pieces at an insane price! It’s the best excuse to shop and not feel too guilty about it. I recently took the 5k challenge at the VERO MODA store as most of you would have seen on my stories and I was amazed to see the looks I could create under that amount.

One of them I have styled here with the yellow trench coat and striped cullotes. It’s super comfortable yet very stylish. I like to add a throw on over my top just for layering, and I couldn’t let go of this color.
